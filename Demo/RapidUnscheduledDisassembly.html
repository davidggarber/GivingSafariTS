Musk and Bezos have been hogging all the glory! That’s according to your boss, a billionaire who also dabbles in the spaceflight business. She’s getting tired of the expense of incorrectly assembled rockets employing lithobraking instead of returning gracefully, and re-usably, to Earth. The rapid unscheduled disassembly of each rocket has been spectacular. Your predecessors have been unceremoniously fired as a result. You’re charged with putting this rocket together, paying close attention to the inside wiring, suspected of being the cause of previous failures. There should be no stray cables that don’t terminate at a control box. The finished rocket has the owner and founder’s motto painted on the side.
