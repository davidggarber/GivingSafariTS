Five friends each have a food truck and contracted to be the only concessions at an outdoor festival. They want to maximize their availability to the crowd, so they each agree to park their trucks in a different row, column, and region on the festival grounds, with each block on the grounds identified by a letter of the alphabet. They further agree not to park on a block directly next to each other, even diagonally.
