💻 For the best experience, use a desktop or laptop computer.
Tablets, such as iPad, work ok.
Mobile phones struggle to see enough of a puzzle, especially with a virtual keyboard.
⌨️ Think of these as (augmented) paper puzzles.
If you see an underline or a box, you can likely type into it.
📝 Take notes.
Like a paper puzzle, take notes anywhere on the page.
Ctrl+click to start, or re-enter a note. Try it here!
🖱️ Some puzzles have richer interactions.
If you see dots to connect, or words to select, try using your mouse.
Try drag-dropping things that look like puzzle pieces.
🔣 Recognize common codes.
Does it look like Morse Code? or Braille? Semaphore? or Pig Pen?
The most common code is 1➜A, 2➜B, … 26➜Z.
Several puzzles offer a link to a helpful decoder (as in the corner below).
⁉️ Submit guesses.
Every puzzle has a "Submit" area at the bottom.
It will confirm correct answers, and mark those puzzles complete on the home page.
In some cases, it will confirm intermediate answers. No penalty for guessing.
🧩 Meta puzzles.
A meta puzzle is one whose inputs come from solving other puzzles.
As you solve the feeders that lead to it, progress will show on the home page.
Those materials should then auto-populate on the meta puzzle's page.
🖨️ Want a printout?
PDFs for all puzzles in a round can be found from the home page.
If printing the current puzzle from your browser, use these settings:
   ✔️ Margins = None    ✔️ Background graphics = checked
🪵 Log in!
Logging in allows the organizers to see how many puzzles you've solved.
Player name is arbitrary, and is not linked to your identity.
But player name + avatar🐶 should be unique, or the system may merge you.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Join a team!
Team names are also arbitrary. Try to be unique.
All members of a team can see each other's progress on puzzles.
Answers are not shared, so you can each enjoy every puzzle.
But, if anyone unlocks materials for a meta, those are unlocked for all.
🌟 Vote!
Let us know which puzzles are fun, boring, easy, or hard.
A rating UI is at the top of every puzzle page.
