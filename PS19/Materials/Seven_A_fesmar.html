Materials Packet 1

Alabaster Statue

The massive statue of Zeus glares from this high rocky perch, down at the amphitheater to the west. The statue is pure alabaster with gold accents. To the south is the side wall of a temple to Artemis. There is a sheer drop to the north, overlooking a green promenade. To the east is a narrow opening into a cave.

You can go east.

Blue Pond

The vivid blue pond in the forest clearing is filled with darting fish, a few lily pads, and some frogs. A stream flows into the pond from the north, bordered by a brick path. The path continues south down a hill. A dirt trail goes west into the forest. There are stairs to a temple to the east.

You can go north, south, east, or west.
