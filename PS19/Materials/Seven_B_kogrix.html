Materials Packet 2

Maroon Mausoleum

This small walled cemetery is dominated by an ornate mausoleum of deep maroon, filigreed with gold. There is a lonely vase beside the closed mausoleum entrance, sealed tightly shut. Loose green cobblestones form a path to the east and you can just see a deep crevasse over the wall to the north.

You can go east.

Sable Cave

Black dripping stalactites drape overhead, splashing on the uneven ground of the blackened limestone in this cave. You can see a switch on the wall to the north, beside an open stone slab door. A narrow passage departs to the west, behind which you can see a massive foot and an iron gate stands across the exit to the south.

You can go north, south, or west.
