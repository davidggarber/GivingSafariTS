Chartreuse Hanging Gardens

Standing walls of brilliant chartreuse make a labyrinth of this garden. Greenery sprouts out of the walls at every level, leafy profusions hiding much of the ornate carvings etched into the walls. There is a hint of a light blue building visible to the south.

You can go south.

Red Leafy Forest

A forest edges up to the rocky beach here. Each tree has white bark with leaves of brilliant red. Leaves are covering the forest floor. There is a brick path up a hill to the north, while the beach continues both east and west. Further west you can see a lighthouse at the end of a jetty.

You can go east, west, or north.
