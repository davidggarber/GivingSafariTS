Materials Packet 3

Orange Cream Lighthouse

There is a tall lighthouse at the end of a jetty here, painted in orange and cream. It reminds you of sherbet. Yum. There is a ladder on the side of the lighthouse, up to a beacon enclosure. From atop the ladder you can see the red foliage to the east and a gazebo atop the escarpment to the north, but there is no path that direction.

You can go east.

Turquoise Gazebo

A trail ends at a stone gazebo standing proudly in the middle of a clearing in the forest. Its walls and roof are made of large plates of intricately veined turquoise. The forest opens to pillars of hanging greenery to the north. You can see a hint of water through the trees to the east.

You can go north or east.

White Sand Beach

The sand on this secluded beach gleams brilliantly in the sun, while the sea laps gently to the east. On the cliff above to the west rises a yellow-hued stone structure. A causeway to the south demarcates the end of the white sand beach. To the north is an iron gate into a cave. At the end of the causeway is a massive figure pointing out to sea.

You can go north or south.
