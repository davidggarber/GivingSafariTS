Materials Packet 5

Indigo Pyramid

A massive pyramid made from stones of deepest indigo rises into the sky here. A tightly packed, green cobblestone street ends at the pyramid. There is a crevasse to the north with no possible crossings. There is a stone door in the cliff face leading into a cave to the south.

You can go west or south.

Cerulean Amphitheater

A brick path runs north and south along a cheery stream. An arched bridge crosses the stream. It is a striking cerulean blue and connects to a small amphitheater made of white marble and cerulean bench seats. Raised east above the seats is a massive statue of Zeus, with a raised fist about to strike down at the audience with a glare.

You can go north or south.
