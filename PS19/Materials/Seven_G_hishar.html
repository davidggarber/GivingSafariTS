Materials Packet 7

Scarlet Colossus

There is a causeway from the cliff out to a massive statue, beautifully carved of a rich scarlet-tinged marble. It stands boldly, one hand shielding its eyes and the other pointing out to sea. The causeway divides the beaches. To the north is a white sand beach and to the west is a rocky beach with a large forge in the cliff.

You can go west or north.

Aquamarine Aqueduct

A crevasse cuts across the land to the north, where there is a waterfall spilling down the cliff on the opposite side of the crevasse. The water lands in an aquamarine aqueduct that flows across the crevasse to spill into a stream that runs southward beside a brick path. Crossing under the aqueduct, a loose green cobblestone path runs east and west.

You can go east, west, or south.
