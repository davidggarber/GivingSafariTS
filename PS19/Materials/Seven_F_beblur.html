Yellowing Temple

Stairs lead from the west up to this yellowing temple, dedicated to the goddess Artemis. The building is ringed in columns, but it is open to the elements on its western wall. at the back of the temple rises a large column from which the unclad upper torso of the goddess rises, arms outstretched.

You can go west.

Dun Forge

This rocky beach runs east and west. Surf crashes and sprays up on the rocks. The briny smell of the sea washes on the breeze. There is a massive forge built against the cliff face here, with another building on the cliff above to the north. To the west you can see a red forest and to the east you see a red stone statue at the end of a causeway.

You can go east or west.
