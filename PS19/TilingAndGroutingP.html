Cut the 36 tiles at the dotted lines and use the word pairs at the tile edges to rearrange them.

Tiles do not rotate. Word pairs read left to right or top to bottom.

Letters are underlined only to clarify their correct orientation.

Finally, read off the 36 large centre letters to get a clue for the three word final answer.
