Note: Knowledge of the movie Dude, Where’s My Car? is neither necessary nor useful to solve this puzzle.

Each of Christie, Jesse, Musclehead, Nelson, Pizzacoli, Tommy, Wanda, Wilma, and Zoltan has recklessly destroyed exactly one of the buildings numbered 1, 2, 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, 24, and 26, leading to the Redmond campus’s current state of disarray. The following clues will help you determine who destroyed which building and the answer is these nine people’s slogan! For your convenience, a logic grid is given below.

  1. Wanda and Wilma both destroyed single-digit buildings.
  2. There is exactly one person who destroyed a building whose number is one less than the number of letters in that person’s name.
  3. Jesse and Pizzacoli both destroyed prime-numbered buildings.
  4. The buildings destroyed by Christie and Musclehead are consecutively numbered.
  5. The building Nelson destroyed is a multiple of Wanda’s building and a factor of Zoltan’s building.
  6. Wilma’s building number is smaller than Nelson’s building number.
  7. The numbers of the buildings destroyed by Jesse and Nelson sum to the number of a building destroyed by another of the nine people.
